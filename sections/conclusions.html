Workshop Conclusions

The MULTITTRUST workshop at the HAI conference 2023 concluded on the 4th of December 2023. This edition brought together experts from diverse fields such as psychology, computer science, and AI, to explore the dynamics of trust in human-AI interactions. Key highlights included innovative approaches to enhancing AI transparency, methods to measure and build trust in AI systems, and discussions on ethical considerations in AI development.

Participants engaged in sessions, sharing case studies and research findings that emphasized the importance of interdisciplinary collaboration in this field.

We are pleased to announce that the proceedings from the workshop are now available on CEUR Workshop Proceedings. You can access them at CEUR-WS Vol 3634. We extend our gratitude to CEUR for their sponsorship and support in making these resources available to a wider audience.
